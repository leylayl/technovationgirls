Identifying Problems

Step 1: Understanding your surroundings

Start with Communities

Every innovation begins with a need. The best place to look is within your own community. Every society has unmet needs waiting for a solution.

Geographic

People in the same neighborhood or city.

Interest-based

People sharing hobbies, goals, or passions.

Pro Tip: Once you find a problem, categorize it. This helps in choosing the right tools to solve it.
