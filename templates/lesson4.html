CONSIDER IMPACT

How to choose the right problem to solve

To consider the impact of solving problems, analyze these four critical elements:

Scale

Is it a big problem? Does it affect a significant number of people?

Publicity

Has the problem appeared in national or public news recently?

Innovation

Is there room for innovation? Are there already companies working on it?

Support

Are there national foundations or organizations interested in this issue?

Note: High impact means your solution can change many lives, making it more attractive to investors.
