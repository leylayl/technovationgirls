Partnering with Orgs

Building a network of strategic allies

Partners are: People or companies who work together to achieve common goals.

Deep Connection

A partner is more than just someone who is helping you with a project; they are invested in the outcome.

Mutual Value

They help you meet your goals, but they must also get something valuable in exchange.

Tech Access

One way to reward partners is giving them the ability to use your premium app for free.

Incentives

Value can be financial, but it can also be through shared publicity or shared resources.
