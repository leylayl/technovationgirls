LESSON 08: BUSINESS PLAN

Your roadmap to a successful and sustainable startup.

A Business Plan is a formal document that defines your goals and how you will achieve them. It's what you show to partners and investors.

1

Executive Summary

A brief overview of your startup's mission, vision, and the core problem you are solving.

2

Market Analysis

Detailed research on your target audience and the competitors you identified in earlier lessons.

3

Operations Plan

Technical workflow of your app and the list of strategic partners required for launch.

4

Financial Plan

Projected costs, funding requirements, and your strategy for reaching profitability.

Final Task

Combine all your notes, research, and tasks from Lesson 01 to 07 into one professional Business Plan document.
