Market Research

Researching Problems

Step 2: Deep dive into the issue

Methodical Research

Once a problem is identified, you must prove its existence. Research helps you understand how many people face this difficulty and how they currently navigate it.

Interviews

Conduct in-depth interviews with potential customers to hear their personal experiences.

Surveys

Gather quantitative data through online forms to see the scale of the problem.

Pro Tip: Avoid asking leading questions. Let people describe their pain points in their own words without bias.
